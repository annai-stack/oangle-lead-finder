The problem

One salesperson. Hundreds of cold leads. All by hand.

  • Oangle sells POS, self-ordering kiosks, order-relay & digital menu boards to multi-outlet F&B chains.
  • Finding the right chain, the right decision-maker, and a reason to call is hours of manual research per lead.
  • The result: shallow outreach, missed accounts, and a pipeline capped by one person's time.
The solution

A pipeline that researches like an analyst — at scale

Feed it your ICP; it returns a ranked spreadsheet of real decision-makers, each with a personalised sales paragraph grounded in live web research.

  • Real contacts — verified names, emails, LinkedIn, seniority.
  • Deep enrichment — company brief, press, how-to-target, CVP, heat rank.
  • Sales-ready — drops straight into the team's existing spreadsheet format.

How the cost falls

Five levers, all behind one flag

  • Model tiering — Sonnet drafts the bulk; Opus only polishes top-tier outreach.
  • Two-tier polish — premium rewrite for 1A/1B leads, no extra web search.
  • Search caps — bound the web-search tokens that dominate cost.
  • Prompt caching — the client profile is re-read at ~0.1×.
  • Batch API — 50% off, runs hundreds of leads overnight.
